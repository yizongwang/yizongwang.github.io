Biography

I am pursuing the Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT, China), advised by Prof. Huadong Ma. I received the B.E. degree in computer science and technology from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2019. My research work focuses on (i) video trasnmisison, e.g., live streaming, volumetric video; (ii) mobile computing; (iii) data mining.
